Ammo Lock & Prioritize is a mod that allows you make a weapon to prioritize or only use 1 type of ammo

originally requested by UsodaKanpeki

by holding an ammo and hovering over a weapon that can use it then right - click to prioritize ammo type
by holding an ammo and hovering over a weapon that can use it then alt + right - click to lock ammo type

you can simply remove this effect by shift and right clicking the weapon
